Why We Chose These Colours

Blue, white and an accent green — colors chosen to communicate trust, transparency and growth.

Blue — Trust

Represents trust, stability and reliability — the foundation of our scoring system.

White — Transparency

Symbolizes openness — every score can be traced to verifiable data.

Green — Growth

Signals growth & opportunity — collaborations users unlock.

  • Data-first Design — badges, charts & concise reports make credibility tangible.
