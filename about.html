About Us

We quantify creator credibility so brands and creators can collaborate with confidence.

Our Philosophy

“Credibility is Currency.” We believe trust is worth more than numbers — our scoring system is transparent and designed to reward real engagement.

Guiding Principles

  • Transparency over secrecy — show the math, not just the score.
  • Empowerment over gatekeeping — give creators tools to grow.
  • Authenticity over hype — reward real audiences.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to become the global standard of trust in the creator economy, where credibility is measurable, visual, and valued. We envision a world where authentic engagement is recognized and rewarded, fostering a more transparent and confident ecosystem for creators and brands alike.
