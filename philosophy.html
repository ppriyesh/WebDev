The Idea

In today’s digital economy, credibility is currency. Brands want to know exactly which creators they can trust, and creators want to prove their worth in a way that is measurable and recognised. Credify bridges this gap by calculating a Social Credibility Score based on real engagement, content quality, and authenticity — and making it visible to both creators and brands.
